# Burger Dash Mission

Burger Dash is a polished, fast, fun, mobile-friendly endless runner with a strong burger-joint identity.

## Product Goal
Ship a game that feels immediately playable and memorable, with clear pressure, satisfying movement, readable obstacles, and a distinct burger-joint world.

## Success Criteria
- The core run is fun within the first 30 seconds.
- Turns feel readable and responsive.
- Obstacles and coins are fair at speed.
- The visible chaser creates pressure without feeling cheap.
- The burger-joint theme is obvious in visuals, UI, and moment-to-moment feel.
- The project can progress autonomously without needing the user to restate the plan each day.

## Non-Negotiables
- Do not rewrite `js/game.js`, `js/track.js`, or `js/player.js` from scratch.
- Prefer additive, localized, testable changes.
- Preserve playability at all times.
- Prioritize fun, readability, and identity before feature sprawl.
